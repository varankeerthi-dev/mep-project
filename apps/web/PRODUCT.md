# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

CEO and management executives overseeing MEP projects across the organisation. Their primary job is to maintain operational clarity — approving expenses, tracking project health, and making informed decisions without digging through spreadsheets or emails.

## Product Purpose

A purpose-built MEP ERP that connects every stage of project delivery — from quotation through execution to financial close — so the leadership team has real-time visibility and control over all operations in one place.

## Positioning

End-to-end project control for MEP contractors, from quotation to financial close, in one platform.

## Brand Personality

Precise, authoritative, confident. The interface earns trust through clarity and accuracy — every number, label, and state is deliberate. Tone is direct and professional, not casual or salesy.

## Anti-references

Must not look like a traditional ERP (SAP, Oracle) — dated grids, cluttered navigation, dense tables without hierarchy. The experience should feel modern and intentional, not like a legacy enterprise tool forced into a browser.

## Design Principles

- **Clarity first**: Every screen answers "what needs my attention?" before showing anything else. Information is surfaced by priority, not by volume.
- **Precision over decoration**: Data is the primary visual material. Layout, spacing, and typography exist to make numbers and statuses legible at a glance.
- **Confident restraint**: Professional doesn't mean boring. Use the brand accent deliberately and sparingly. No superfluous gradients, illustrations, or ornamentation.
- **Stateful surfaces**: Every data view accounts for loading, empty, error, and edge cases. A blank table is a design failure.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. Body text contrast ≥4.5:1. All interactive elements are keyboard-reachable and have visible focus indicators.
